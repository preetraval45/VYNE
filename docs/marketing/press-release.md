## VYNE launches the first AI-native company OS that replaces Slack, Jira, Notion, and Salesforce in one workspace

**[CITY, DATE]** — VYNE today announced the public launch of its AI-native company operating system, a single workspace that replaces eight categories of business software — chat, project management, docs, CRM, accounting, ticketing, ops, and analytics — under a unified AI agent that can read, write, and reason across all of it.

The product is built on a thesis that surprised most observers but lands when you use it: knowledge-work companies don't actually need fifteen disconnected tools. They need one workspace where the AI knows about every deal, every Slack thread, every PR, every invoice, and every customer ticket — and can stitch them together into answers that no point-solution can produce.

"You can ask Vyne 'which customers with overdue invoices have an open support ticket and a deal in the pipeline,' and it answers in three seconds with the right rows highlighted across CRM, support, and finance," said Preet Raval, founder and CEO of VYNE. "That question is impossible to answer in a multi-tool stack without an army of analysts. It's instant when your data lives in one place and an AI can reason over it."

### What's launching today

VYNE ships nine production-grade pillars:

- **Real-time collaborative workspace** — every record (deal, task, doc, invoice) supports inline editing, presence avatars, live cursors, and per-cell co-edit borders.
- **AI agent with tool calling** — the assistant can create, update, query, and delete records across CRM, projects, ops, finance, and contacts. Approval gates protect destructive writes.
- **RAG-grounded answers** — drag a PDF into chat, ask questions, get cited answers with hover previews of the source spans.
- **Skills (multi-step chains)** — save a workflow as `/skill weekly-pipeline-review` and invoke it from any chat.
- **Continuous voice mode** — hands-free Q&A with the AI, complete with barge-in and TTS playback.
- **Huddles** — Discord-style ad-hoc audio rooms in any channel, persistent across navigation.
- **Per-channel chat workflows** — visual trigger-action rules that fire when messages match patterns.
- **Universal Cmd+K** — search every record across every module, with AI fall-through when no exact match exists.
- **SOC 2-ready** — field-level permissions, audit log persistence, RBAC, BYOK, GDPR export/erasure endpoints.
